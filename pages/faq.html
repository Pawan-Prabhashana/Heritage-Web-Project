ProductsHow do I publish a craft?
Add a product with title, category, price, stock and images. When ready, toggle it to Published. You can edit at any time.
ExperiencesHow do I enable workshop booking?
When adding or editing a product, tick Offer an experience and fill the date, duration and max seats. You can add multiple dates later.
PayoutsWhat are the fees & payout cycle?
A transparent platform fee is applied per order/booking. Payouts group weekly; you’ll see statements in your dashboard.
AccountHow does verification work?
We review identity, craft history and samples. Verified profiles get a badge that boosts buyer trust.
